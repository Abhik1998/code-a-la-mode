INPUT
width: the width of OUR SIDE of the restaurant
height: the height of the restaurant
numOurTables: the number of tables accessible to us
numAllTables: the total number of tables in the restaurant
playerX: when x>0, our side of the restaurant. x<0, enemy side
tableX: when x>0, our side of the restaurant. x<0, enemy side
numCustomers: the number of customers currently waiting for food
award: the number of points awarded for delivering the food
item: the food the customer is waiting for

OUTPUT
MOVE x y
USE x y
TAKE x y
DROP x y
WAIT
